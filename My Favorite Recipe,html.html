Strawberry Blueberry Wellness Smoothie

The most delicious and nutritious Smoothie you've tasted

List of Ingredients

  • 1) Strawberries
  • 2) Blueberries (Wild)
  • 3) Apple Blueberry Juice Blend
  • 4) Dates
  • 5) Peanut Butter
  • 6) Almonds
  • 7) Stevia Sweetener
  • 8) Spinach

Steps to prepare the Smoothie

  1. 1) Add 3/4 of a cup of water
  2. 2) 2 cup Strawberries
  3. 3) 1 cup Blueberries
  4. 4) 8 oz Apple Blueberry Juice Blend
  5. 5) 1 serving of Dates
  6. 6) 2 tbsp Peanut Butter
  7. 7) 1 oz Almonds
  8. 8) 1/4 tbsp Stevia
  9. 9) 1 cup Spinach
  10. 10) 1 cup Ice

This is my favorite recipe because its very flavorful, good for drinking after or before a workout, and it can be a healthy meal replacement.
